About Demetra

Named after the ancient goddess of agriculture and harvest, Demetra represents the perfect fusion of traditional farming wisdom and cutting-edge technology. We honor the timeless principles of agriculture while embracing innovation to build the future of farming.

Our mission is to democratize precision agriculture by making advanced crop selection technology accessible to farmers and agronomists worldwide. We believe that data-driven decisions should be at the heart of sustainable and profitable farming.

Our Vision

Building the future of agriculture through innovation and expertise

At Demetra, we envision a world where every farmer has access to cutting-edge technology that optimizes crop selection and maximizes agricultural productivity. Our goal is to bridge the gap between traditional farming practices and modern data science.

Who We Are

We are a passionate team of Computer Science and Physics students from Greece, combining our technical expertise with a deep appreciation for agriculture's vital role in society. Our interdisciplinary background allows us to approach agricultural challenges from both computational and scientific perspectives.

Based in Greece, a country with thousands of years of agricultural heritage, we understand the importance of honoring traditional knowledge while embracing technological advancement. Our team brings together expertise in data analysis, software development, and scientific research to create solutions that truly make a difference for farmers.
